Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how and for what purposes we use the information collected about you via the Save-O Website (referred to below as the "Website") or the Save-O mobile app (referred to below as "the App"). By using the Website and the App and any services we offer via the Website and the App, you are agreeing to be bound by this policy in respect of the information collected about you via this Website and the App.
    2. If you have any queries about the policy, please get in touch and we will do our best to answer your questions.

    Personal information collected

    1. We may collect personal information about you, including but not limited to:
      1. Certain information required to register with the Website and the App including first and last name, your address and date of birth, mobile number and some basic security information;
      2. Your email address and password;
      3. Your credit card details including your credit card number, expiry date and CCV number;
      4. Your payment PIN;
      5. Location details when you access the Website and the App;
      6. Any correspondence between you and us, including messages, in whatever form they may be;
      7. Other information about you that you voluntarily supply to us;
      8. Although it is not compulsory to give us this information, if you do not then you cannot register as a member of the Website and the App, load discount vouchers into your profile or make purchase at a service station.

    Use of this information

    1. We will use this information in order to:
      1. Provide you with relevant and timely information relating to your purchase at participating service stations;
      2. Collect payment from you on behalf of our merchants;
      3. Make general improvements to the Website and the App and the services we offer;
      4. Analyse how customers are making use of the Website and the App;
      5. Present testimonials or reviews which you post to the Website and the App;
      6. Aggregate your usage habits and provide you with directions or other helpful travel information;
      7. For internal administrative duties, investigate complaints and provide customer support;
      8. Notify you of certain offers and new products (subject to your right to unsubscribe or opt-out);
      9. Market and advertise relevant products and services. This may include Save-O and our partners sending you information about products and services that will be relevant to you, by email, SMS, push notifications, postal mail or direct telephone contact. All of the above are subject to your stated preferences; we and our content partners will always make it clear how you can prevent such marketing or communications i.e. opt-out of these uses.

    Sharing this information

    1. Credit or debit card payments will be collected by our payment processor.
    2. We do not store credit card numbers or CVV/CVV2 security numbers on our servers.
    3. We have the facility to re-charge a payment source at a later date for recurring bookings, cancellation fees and other payments that need to be taken to enable the smooth running of the Website.
    4. In order for payments to be processed, you may need to provide some necessary details to our payment processing agent(s). We tell you about this at the point we collect that information on the Website and the App.
    5. We may transfer your personal information to a third party as part of a sale of some or all of our business and assets to any third party or as part of any business restructuring or reorganisation, but we will take steps with the aim of ensuring that your privacy rights continue to be protected.
    6. We may share your personally identifiable information with third parties solely for the purpose of providing the Save-O service to you. If we do this, such third parties' use of your information will be bound by this Privacy Policy.
    7. We may share non-personally identifiable information (such as anonymous usage data, referring/exit pages and URLs, platform and browser types, number of clicks, etc.) with interested third parties to help them understand the usage patterns for the Website and the App.
    8. We may allow third party ad servers or ad networks to advertise on the Website and the App. These third party ad servers or ad networks use technology to send, directly to your browser or app, the advertisements and links that appear on the Website and the App. They automatically receive your IP address when this happens. They may also use other technologies (such as “cookies”) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize the advertising content.
    9. Other than as set out above, we will not disclose any of your personal information without your permission unless we are required by law to do so (for example, if required to do so by a court order or for the purposes of prevention of fraud or other crime).
    10. Information automatically collected from your computer: Log files/IP addresses. When you visit the Website and the App our web server automatically records your IP address. This IP address is not linked to any of your personal information. We may also gather other non-personal information (from which we cannot identify you) such as the type of your internet browser which we use to provide you with a more effective service.
    11. Cookies. When you visit the Website and the App we may store some information (commonly known as a "cookie") on your computer. Cookies are pieces of information that a Website transfers to your hard drive to store and sometimes track information about you. Cookies are specific to the server that created them and cannot be accessed by other servers, which means that they cannot be used to track your movements around the web. Passwords and credit card numbers are not stored in cookies. A cookie helps you get the best out of the Website and the App and helps us to provide you with a more customised service. We use cookies for the following purposes:
      1. Storing your login details so you don’t need to enter your login again if you haven’t logged out;
      2. Storing details about your Website preferences (for instance, where you are based);
      3. Storing details about any locations you have viewed;
      4. Enabling our web server to track your session between pages of the Website and the App and provide a continuity of experience.
      5. You can block or erase cookies from your computer if you want to (your browser's help screen or manual should tell you how to do this), but certain parts of the Website and the App are reliant on the use of cookies to operate correctly and may not work correctly if you set your browser not to accept cookies.
